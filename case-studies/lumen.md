                    <li>
                        <i class="far fa-question-circle"></i>
                        <p><strong>Need for a single unified platform.</strong> The client required one platform capable of integrating predictive maintenance, asset management, energy analytics, and environmental monitoring.</p>

                    <li>
                        <i class="far fa-question-circle"></i>

                    <li>
                        <i class="far fa-question-circle"></i>
                        <p><strong>Scalability across multiple plants.</strong> The solution needed to support thousands of telemetry points across multiple sites without compromising performance.</p>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                        <i class="far fa-question-circle"></i>
                        <p><strong>Long-term operational stability.</strong> In a 24/7 industrial environment, system reliability and uptime were critical.</p>

    <section class="tb-power mb">
        <h2>How ThingsBoard powers Lumen’s Industrial IoT Application?</h2>
        <div class="block">
            <div class="text">
                <h3>Large-scale device integration</h3>
                <p>Over 1,000 IoT sensors and industrial energy meters were integrated into a centralized ThingsBoard platform. Data from production lines, substations, environmental systems, and utility infrastructure is collected in real time through secure gateways and standardized protocols.</p>
                <p>The platform enables unified visualization and historical analytics, transforming raw telemetry into actionable operational insights.</p>
            </div>
            <img src="/images/case-studies/lumen-1.webp" title="Large-scale device integration" alt="Large-scale device integration" width="450" height="240">
        </div>
        <div class="block">
            <div class="text">
                <h3>Personalized dashboards for multiple industrial applications</h3>
                <p>Lumen developed custom dashboards tailored to each operational area:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Predictive Maintenance Monitoring</li>
                    <li>Asset Performance Tracking</li>
                    <li>Energy Consumption & Demand Analysis</li>
                    <li>Temperature and Environmental Control</li>
                    <li>Utility Optimization</li>
                </ul>
                <p>Role-based dashboards ensure that plant managers, maintenance teams, and executives access relevant data aligned with their responsibilities.</p>
            </div>
            <img src="/images/case-studies/lumen-2.webp" title="Personalized dashboards for multiple industrial applications" alt="Personalized dashboards for multiple industrial applications" width="450" height="240">
        </div>
        <div class="block">
            <div class="text">
                <h3>Hybrid deployment: cloud & on-premise architecture</h3>
                <p>Due to strict IT and cybersecurity requirements, the client required flexible deployment options. ThingsBoard’s support for both Cloud and On-Premise configurations allowed Lumen to design a secure architecture aligned with corporate IT policies. This hybrid capability was a key factor in successfully delivering and scaling the project.</p>
            </div>
            <img src="/images/case-studies/lumen-3.webp" title="Hybrid deployment: cloud & on-premise architecture" alt="Hybrid deployment: cloud & on-premise architecture" width="450" height="240">
        </div>
        <div class="block">
            <div class="text">
                <h3>Long-term stability in a 24/7 industrial environment</h3>
                <p>The platform has been operating continuously for more than four years, supporting high-frequency telemetry across thousands of data points without performance degradation.</p>
                <p>The system architecture allows seamless onboarding of new devices, additional production areas, and future expansion plans.</p>
            </div>
            <img src="/images/case-studies/lumen-4.webp" title="Long-term stability in a 24/7 industrial environment" alt="Long-term stability in a 24/7 industrial environment" width="450" height="240">
